Visitup Bologna - Android app

Tourist guide app of Bologna. There are an historical tour with audioguides and an aperitivo tour.
The historical tour is a full historical tour of Bologna in 3 days in which you will see the most beautiful parts of the city.
The aperitivo tour is a list of the main bar and restaurants of Bologna with reviews, phone number, location and opening hours, divided in the 4 main area of the city centre.
