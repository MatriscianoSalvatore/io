Mobile Developer at Deloitte, Milano

The first project I worked on in Deloitte was the Android native app of a prestigious Italian automotive manufacturer.
I really liked it because it was a very cool and famous car brand and the app was very nice with cool features. The main purpose of the app was to control the connected car, so open and close it, switch the lights and the air conditioning on and off etc, everything via app. I worked on fixing some bugs, as fixing the Facebook login that was not working anymore, fixing the map view for the geofencing that was not displaying correctly etc.
For this project I used Java, Kotlin, Android Studio, Git, Bitbucket, Fork and Microsoft Planner and I focused my attention and studied how Design Patterns can be applied to Android apps and the differences between MVC, MVP and MVVM, how Clean Architecture can be applied to Android and a little bit about Kotlin.
